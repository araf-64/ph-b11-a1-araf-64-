Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

New Bangladesh is embracing cutting-edge technologies and fostering a culture of creativity to drive economic progress and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Prioritizing eco-friendly initiatives, New Bangladesh is committed to sustainable development, ensuring a balance between growth and environmental preservation.

Infrastructure

Massive investments in modern infrastructure are transforming the landscape, improving connectivity, and supporting industrial expansion across the nation.

Empowerment

Focused on empowering its people, New Bangladesh is enhancing education, creating job opportunities, and promoting inclusivity to build a robust and equitable society.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future for all citizens.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
